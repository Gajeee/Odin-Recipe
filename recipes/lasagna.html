Lasagna

Description

A rich and hearty lasagna with layers of meat sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook noodles.
  2. Prepare sauce.
  3. Layer ingredients.
  4. Bake until golden.
